Privacy Policy

This policy describes what data the Weekset app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. We collect only what the app needs to work.

Data we collect

  • Account information — your phone number and name, provided when you register.
  • Event data — the events you create and their details.
  • Technical identifiers — your user ID.

Device permissions and why we request them

Weekset asks for a permission only at the moment you use the feature that needs it. Every permission is optional, and you can change your choice at any time in your device settings.

Camera Used only to scan event QR codes so you can open an event without typing a link. Requested at the moment you start scanning.
Face ID / Touch ID Biometric sign-in is optional and used only for fast access to your account. Biometric data is processed entirely by your device's operating system and is never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

How we use data

We use your data solely to operate Weekset's core functionality: creating and managing events and generating their QR codes. We do not use your data for advertising and we do not build profiles of you.

We do not sell your data. Weekset contains no analytics services, no advertising networks, and no third-party tracking tools.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your data only for as long as your account exists. When an account is deleted, the events associated with it are permanently removed and cannot be restored.

You can send any data-related request, including a deletion request, to weekset.support@gmail.com.

Your rights and contact

You may request access to the data we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or withdraw your consent to its processing at any time. For any question about this policy or your data, write to weekset.support@gmail.com.
